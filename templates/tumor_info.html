Types of Brain Tumors

  • Glioma: Tumors that begin in the glial cells of the brain or spine.
  • Meningioma: Tumors that form on membranes covering the brain and spinal cord.
  • No Tumor: The scan shows a normal brain without tumors.
  • Pituitary: Tumors located in the pituitary gland, affecting hormonal functions.
